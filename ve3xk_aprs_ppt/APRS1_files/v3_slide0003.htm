The Bell 103 Type Modem
300 baud (bits per second) data rate
 Frequency Shift Keying with 200 Hz shift
At one end, modem transmits on Frequency Pair F1
(Mark 1270 Hz, Space 1070 Hz) and receives on
Frequency Pair F2 (Mark 2225 Hz, Space 2025 Hz)
At other end, operation is opposite
This is Full Duplex, but amateur packet uses only
half-duplex (sequential T and R)
