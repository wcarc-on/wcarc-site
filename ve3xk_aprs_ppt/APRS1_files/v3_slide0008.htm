Digital Repeater (Digipeater)
The digipeater is an essential building block in the
packet network as it extends the range of all terminal
nodes.
Unlike voice repeaters that retransmit everything
they receive, the digipeater temporarily stores and
then retransmits (repeats) only the packet radio
transmissions that are specifically addressed for
routing through that digipeater, or aliases (eg WIDE,
RELAY, etc) accepted by that repeater.
