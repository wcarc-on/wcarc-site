Tellurometer Specs - Electrical
• Model:          MRA3 Mk II
• Frequency:   10.025GHz – 10.450GHz
• Power:          20-50mw (ERP is abt 12.8w)
• Beam Width: 9 deg @ 3dB point
• Mod Bandwidth: 100KHz
• IF:                33MHz
• Full Duplex only
• Completely solid state except Klystron
• 12vdc
• Amateur Band is 10.0GHz – 10.5GHz
