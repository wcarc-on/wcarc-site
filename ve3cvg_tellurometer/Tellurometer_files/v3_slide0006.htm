Where do I get one?
• Flea Markets
• Garage Sales
• Surplus stores
• Contact VE3FN, VE3BBM, VE2JWH, VE3CVG
to buy or borrow
• Expect to spend $25 - $100 ea for a working unit
• Make sure that you get a model that is on the same
frequency as your other one.
• Buy in working pairs if possible.
• Get handset, cables and connectors or be ready to
do some mods.
