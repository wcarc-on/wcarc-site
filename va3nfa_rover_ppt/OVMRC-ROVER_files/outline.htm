Grid Locators Explained
Position on Earth based on Lat/Long
Earth divided into 324 large areas called fields that are 10 degree Lat and 20 degrees Long in size
Each Field is divided into 100 “grid locators” that represent 1 degree by 2 degree
Ottawa area east of Carp is in “FN25”
West of Carp is in “FN15”

Contest Scoring
Score: Total QSO points x Multipliers
QSO Points per QSO:
50/144: 1 point
222/432: 2 points
903/1296: 4 points
2.3GHz & up: 8 points
Multipliers
Total of unique grid locators worked per band

Safety First!
Stop and Operate is safest!

Power
