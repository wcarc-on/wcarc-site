VO1NO Antenna Party July 3, 2006

On July 3rd, 2006, Al (VO1NO) had an antenna raising party and had a good turnout of helping hands. Al gave us a working demonstration of the falling derek method of raising a tower. Amazing operation to watch. We had a refreshing drink at half time and a BBQ after the hard work was over.
