Barney, VA3BGB, Secretary, moved that the minutes of the 10 September meeting be accepted as published. Graham VE3BYT seconded the motion and the motion was passed.

OLD BUSINESS

Beacon Report

Doug, VE3XK reported that Sean Huntley VE3HXP had informed him that he had discovered that the modification of the available radio for use as a 2m beacon was not technically possible. Doug mentioned that all other hardware has been prepared for installation and is concerned that winter conditions would soon interfere with the installation still requiring a 2m beacon. Much discussion ensued with six members offering to provide donations toward the purchase of the necessary hamtronics components with Al VO1NO/VE3 offering to cover any remaining costs.

Balloon Project

Graham, VE3BYT reported that very few people have become involved. The group has acquired an ATV transmitter with much remaining to be done prior to launch which is planned for mid November.

Rick, VE3CVG - no report

Webmaster

Rick VE3CVG is continuing his efforts toward the transfer of the clubs website domain name. Rick is working with Manfred to accomplish this transfer. The constitution is now available on the website.
