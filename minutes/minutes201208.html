Treasurer's Report:

General Report and Announcements:

Beacons
Doug Leach (VE3XK) reported that the WCARC beacons continue to operate with no problems.

Grid Expedition
The President reported that he had been contacted by Dana VE3KH who requested a description of the club's recent Grid Expedition for his VHF column in TCA. He reported that he has provided it and noted to members that the original draft was modified to protect club "trade secrets"!

Contest Club Ontario
The CCO Annual BBQ takes place August 25 at the QTH of VE3EJ, Grassie (see Grimsby), ON. The Newmarket Hamfest is the same day.

Ottawa Hamfest
The Hamfest takes place as usual in Carp - this year on Saturday, September 8th. WCARC will have a table.

Presentations
Al Penny (VO1NO) delivered a presentation on NVIS (Near Vertical Incidence Skywave) based on a presentation developed by G3LEQ.
Andy Hart (VE3NVK) gave us a short presentation on the Raspberry Pi computer used for a variety of projects including state-of-the-art DIY D-STAR repeaters or hot spots.
