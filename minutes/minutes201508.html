Reports / Project Updates

Beacons
Doug Leach delivered a short presentation on the installation of the beacons at their new location. The installation went well and quickly and everything is now operating. Power outputs now are 1.5 w. at 2m and .5 w on 6m, 1.25m, and 70cm. Members and other beacon users have reported good signals in the local area and the 6m beacon has been heard in Florida.

D-Star
Rick Bandala (VE3CVG) reported that D-Star systems are working well across the region. For D-Star users at the meeting he noted that there will be some changes in how linking will be handled. All D-Star users have been notified.

New Business

Project Proposals
At the next meeting several members will present project proposals for the membership to consider.

Announcements
The ARRL Microwave Contest will take place of two weekends - one in mid August (August 15 - 16) and the other in mid September. Two WCARC members Ray Perrin (VE3FN) and Dean Denter (VA3CDD) will participate and several club members will assist them during the contest.

There will be a QCWA event at the Aviation museum August 12. It will include a Fox hunt and other events. Everyone is welcome.

Show and tell
Dave Conn showed a board used to take advantage of the abundance of computer power supplies and make it easier to use them for electrons work. Boards are available at By-a-Pie for 15. He described the polymer devices that provide over current protection and the science.

Presentation
A video presentation on Broadband Hamnet developed by Bert Zauhar (VE2ZAZ) was shown at the meeting. Bert was unable to attend to present, in person. Rick Bandla (VE3CVG) and Ying Hum (VA3YH) assisted in the presentation and the Q and A session, which went on for an extended time. Members were enthusiastic about the effectiveness of the comprehensive presentation.


Adjournment
The President stated that there being no further business the meeting was adjourned at 22:30 hours.
