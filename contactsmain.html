    Club Call Signs

    The Club call signs are issued by Industry Canada and include VE3WCC and VE3DVQ. The official contact for matters related to the Club call signs is the WCARC President.

    List

    The West Carleton Amateur Radio Club web based forum or list is actually a yahoo group called the wcclist. NOTE: This is a restricted group located at https://ca.groups.yahoo.com/neo/groups/wcclist/info

    Membership must be approved by one of the moderators. To send a message to our list, you MUST be a member of the list.
